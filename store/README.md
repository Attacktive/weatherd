### Full description (4000 chars max)

```
Weatherd turns your home screen into a living sky.

It paints a procedural scene from real conditions — clear sun, drifting clouds, rain, snow, fog, and thunderstorms — and updates as the weather changes. Dawn, day, dusk, and night shift the light so the wallpaper feels like the world outside your window.

Set it once as a live wallpaper. Weatherd refreshes on a schedule you choose (15 minutes to 6 hours), using your device location or a city you search for. An in-app preview shows the same scene the wallpaper will draw.

What you get
• Live wallpaper driven by real weather data
• Procedural scenes: sun, clouds, rain, snow, sleet, fog, thunder
• Day phases: dawn, day, dusk, night
• Device location or manual city search
• Configurable refresh interval
• Lightweight companion app with a live preview

Weather data from Open-Meteo. No account and no API key required.

Weatherd is a wallpaper — not a forecast app. It does not show hourly charts or severe-weather alerts; it quietly keeps your background in sync with the sky.
```
